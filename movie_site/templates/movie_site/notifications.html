🏅 How to Earn Badges 2 days ago

Want to collect badges? Here's how to earn them and track your progress:

🍿 Watch movies and TV shows: The more you watch, the more progress you make!
🧩 Complete quizzes: Each completed weekly quiz gives you points towards badges
🎯 Special achievements: Some badges are awarded for milestones like watching 10, 50, or 100 movies
⭐ Daily engagement: Regular visits and interactions earn you bonus points
Where to find your badges:
1. Go to Home → Profile (click on your username)
2. Click on "Your Badges" in the dropdown menu
3. See all your earned badges and track your progress!

Keep watching, exploring, and collecting! Each badge tells a story of your movie journey. The more you engage, the more badges you'll unlock. 🌟
